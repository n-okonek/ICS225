About Page Design Concepts

Main Concepts:

This page is pretty straight forward. When trying to think about what might go on an about page for a radio station I looked at pages for stations that were terrestrial and I noticed they all had some pretty similar things. Things like when the station began, mission statement, a slogan, what the format of the station is, and other miscellaneous things. I wanted to keep this within the pirate theme so terms like "Set Sail" were perfect for when the station began.
